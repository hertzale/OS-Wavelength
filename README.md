# Match My Freq

Match My Freq is an intuition-based game inspired by the popular TikTok intuition game and the board game **Wavelength**. Players compete to see how well they can align their thinking by guessing where specific ideas or items fall on a spectrum of polar opposites.

## 🎮 Game Overview
The goal of the game is to "match the frequency" of your partner. One player acts as the **Psychic**, who knows the target location on a spectrum, and the other acts as the **Surmiser**, who must guess that location based on a single clue.

## 🚀 Game Mechanics

### 1. The Setup
- The game consists of various categories (spectrums) representing two polar opposites (e.g., "Underrated Skill" vs. "Overrated Skill").
- A randomizer selects a spectrum from the available categories at the start of each round.

### 2. Roles
Roles swap every round to ensure both players get a chance to provide clues and guess:
- **Round 1 & 3:** Player 1 is the **Psychic**, Player 2 is the **Surmiser**.
- **Round 2 & 4:** Player 2 is the **Psychic**, Player 1 is the **Surmiser**.
- The game typically lasts for **4 to 5 rounds**.

### 3. Turn Structure
- **Psychic's Turn:** Secretly selects or receives a random Target Level on the scale and provides a specific clue that fits that position.
- **Surmiser's Turn:** Receives the clue and must input a hypothesis (guessed target number) on where the clue sits on the spectrum.

### 4. Scoring System
Points are awarded based on how close the Surmiser's guess is to the Psychic's target:
- **3 Points:** Exact match.
- **2 Points:** Off by 1 increment.
- **1 Point:** Off by 2 increments.
- **0 Points:** Anything further.

## 📊 Example Spectrums
The game includes a wide variety of fun and relatable categories, such as:
- **First Date Spots:** Terrible First Date Spot — Perfect First Date Spot
- **Social Red Flags:** Green Flag — Red Flag
- **Communication:** Ghosting — Communicating
- **Lifestyle:** Bed Rotting — Productive
- **Parenting:** Strict Parent — Lenient Parent
- **Vibe Check:** Conyo Energy — Kanal Humor

## 🎨 UI Layout
The application interface includes:
- **Title Section:** Displaying "Match My Freq".
- **Role Indicator:** Clearly stating who is the Psychic and who is the Surmiser.
- **Instruction Guide:** Helping players navigate their turns.
- **The Scale:** A visual representation of the spectrum.
- **Scoreboard:** Tracking points awarded per turn and overall total points.
- **Reveal Mechanism:** A way to hide the scale during the Psychic's turn and reveal it for the results.
